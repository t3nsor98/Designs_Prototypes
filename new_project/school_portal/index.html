Our Mission

At PraxisForge, we believe that the future of innovation begins in the classroom—and more importantly, in a connected digital classroom. Our mission is to equip school students from classes 6 to 10 with future-ready technical skills through engaging, hands-on online learning experiences.

Through our 100% remote programs, students dive into the world of technology, data science, and innovation with the guidance of expert mentors from leading tech giants. We don't just teach theory—we empower students to build, collaborate, and present real-world projects, all from the convenience of their homes or school labs.

By partnering with schools, we aim to nurture a generation of confident creators and critical thinkers who are ready for the digital age.
